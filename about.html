About

Magic Squirrel Games is an indie game publishing and development company run by game developer and enthusiast Evan Yang.

I first began publishing game development content on my personal YouTube channel, Magic00Squirrel, before moving to the Magic Squirrel Games YouTube channel in March of 2024. This website was launched the same year.

The games I make are created in Unity and target a variety of platforms, including Android, iOS, PC, and potentially consoles in the future.

My first experience making games was in the web-based block-coding tool Scratch. I made my first game in Unity in 2018 by following a tutorial series on the YouTube channel Brackeys.

The name is based on the Xbox 360 gamertag I made for myself years ago. It comes from the children's book series Origami Yoda.
