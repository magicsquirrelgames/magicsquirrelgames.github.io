Frog Hop Privacy Policy

Effective 8-29-2025

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application. By playing Frog Hop, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information we collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, address, or email. However, the game may collect the following types of information automatically through third-party services:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, language, and region.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the game, including session length, gameplay events, and in-game interactions.
  • Advertising Data: Through Unity Ads, we may collect data such as device identifiers and information about ads viewed or clicked.

2. How we use information

We use the collected information to:

  • Improve gameplay experience and performance.
  • Show relevant advertisements through Unity Ads.

3. Third-party services

This game uses Unity Ads (provided by Unity Technologies) to display optional ads. Unity Ads may collect and use data as described in their own privacy policy.

These services may use tracking technologies (such as cookies or device identifiers) to provide targeted advertising and measure ad performance.

4. Data retention

This game retains collected data only as long as necessary to operate and improve. Data collected by Unity Ads is managed according to their retention policies.

5. Your privacy choices

Depending on your device and platform, you may be able to:

  • Limit ad tracking in your device settings.
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising through your platform (e.g., iOS or Android settings).

6. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted here with the updated effective date.
